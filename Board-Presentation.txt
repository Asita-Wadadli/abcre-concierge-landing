Hemisphere Claw Agency proposes a strategic partnership to launch the **A.B.C.R.E. Constitutional Concierge** — an AI-powered constitutional guide that will:

✅ Democratize access to constitutional knowledge

**The product is built and ready to launch.** We seek board approval and partnership agreement.

## THE OPPORTUNITY

### The Problem
For too long, the Constitution has been a document accessible only to lawyers and politicians. Citizens don't know their rights, can't participate meaningfully in democracy, and constitutional reform lacks broad public engagement.

### The Solution
An AI-powered chatbot that provides instant, 24/7 access to the Constitution in plain English, with African-centered perspectives.

**Live Demo:** @abcre_constitution_bot on Telegram
